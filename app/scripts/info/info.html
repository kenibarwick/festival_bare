Careful, now!

Your children are your responsibility, please supervise them appropriately, and have fun together!

Festival Hosts in yellow tabards are here for your comfort and safety – grab one if you need any help or have any questions.

The First Aid & Lost Children tent and Security can be found by the main gate. Both are open 24 hours.

Unsupervised children will not be allowed to leave the main festival site to access the woods, shower block or camper van fields  without a roaming child wristband (available from Front of House).

Camp carefully – leave plenty of room between tents and please, only one gazebo per encampment.

Fire points are shown on the site map.  No fires are allowed in the camp site.

Use camping stoves outside to guard against fire and carbon-monoxide poisoning.

Leave the music to us – no sound systems in the camp sites, please.

Keep hands clean – wild animals don’t clean up after themselves.  

Hot water for hand washing can be found in the permanent toilet blocks.

Keep the festival tidy – use the litter and recycling bins provided and keep cigarette butts off the floor.

In case of emergency dial 999. We are at: The Hop Farm, Maidstone Rd, Paddock Wood, Tonbridge, Kent TN12 6PY
